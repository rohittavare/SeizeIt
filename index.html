How it works

Seize It is an online platform allowing high schoolers to gain internship opportunities by streamlining the process of finding and joining research programs of professors in need of highly qualified students. Our integrated Firebase web server handles the requests and user information. The easy to use UI helps both professors and highschoolers connect faster and avoids the typical spam students send to professors. In many circumstances, a student may never receive emails back and be unaware of the reason. However, with our platform we can assure that students are connected to professors who actually may need help with their current projects or who are willing to help out students with their respective interests.

Who We Are

We are a group of high school students from Cupertino High who are passionate about computer science and helping others. We love problem solving, and strive to solve challenges that we face both in and out of school. Through the development of Seize It we hope to open highschoolers across the nation to take part in the research projects that will shape our present and future.
